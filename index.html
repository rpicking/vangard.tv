What is Vangard?

Vangard is your own personal netflix. Combining multiple services like requesting new media, calendared air dates, and notifications when new movies and shows are added, Vangard sets out to give you a simple, easy to use entertainment experience. Now that you're here, lets jump right in.

Status

To check the status of a service, look for the colored status indicator.

For Example:

Up and running, everything is working as expected. Movies and TV Shows are all streamable.

Service works but minor issues like slow speeds, few items missing, etc.

Service is down completely, missing significant amounts of data or plex server has no internet access.

To report an issue with a service that shows a green indicator, simply navigate to the options menu and fill out the report form.

Plex

Overview

Watch all of you favorite shows and movies in stunning HD right from the comfort of your home. Wih the upcoming Vangard Plex Cloud Server, watch your shows anywhere without buffering or slowdowns.

Running Plex

To start using plex, simply click the start plex button below, login with your plex.tv account information and begin watching.
To check the status of plex look for the green dot up at the top.

Start Plex

Requests

Overview

Finally an easy to use solution for requesting new movies and tv shows. Gone are the days of texting or having to call to get a new show added. With this easy to use system, simply login with your plex username and password and your ready to go

Movies

To request a new movie, simply search for the movie in question, and click request. Depending on your status, a request may be auto-approved, or require approval from an admin before being added which may take additional time.

TV Shows

Requesting a tv show is much like requesting a movie. The only difference being you have the option to select which season(s) you wish to add. Adding the latest or all season(s) will enable all subsequent episodes to be added automatically.

Notifications

Overview

One of the best things about using the slack chat/notifications is not only are you able to get notifications on newly added movies and TV shows, you can do it all on your favorite devices.

On the computer or phone you can use the slack app to view notifications or chat with other members of the Vangard Network.

Don't want to be tied down to an app? Head over to vangardnetwork.slack.com and login to stay up to date whenever and wherever you want.

Because all of these devices are linked with your account, you can change sounds, notification timing, etc. from any device and it will sync to all of them.

Channels

The Vangard Network slack team has multiple channels to better organize notifications by media type.

  • #general - General chat for random discussion and announcements
  • #plex - Uptime/down of the plex server
  • #movies - Movies added to the plex library
  • #tvshows - Episodes added to the plex library

An issue that some of you might have noticed with the current notification system for tv shows is that when whole seasons are added you get spammed with a ton of notifications all at once. I don't have an immediate fix but do plan on fixing it in the somewhat near future

Upcoming Features

Plex Sync

Using plex's built in sync features, you will be able to select movies, tv shows or sports to download and be able to view offline. Because this feature relies heavily on my home server bandwidth, this will have a limited release, most likely in the form of limited download speeds, or item counts. After the Vangard Plex Cloud server is online and operational, the limits will be removed and you will be able to fully enjoy the feature.

ETA: 1-2 weeks for limited access.
Full access after Plex Cloud

Plex in the Cloud

While the process has already been started, which you may have noticed with more buffering in plex than usual, this process will take anywhere from 1 to 3 months to upload everything to the cloud. When its done you can expect to rarely, if at all, see any buffering or slowdowns and will be able to stream anywhere anytime to your hearts content. I will be experimenting with this feature for a few months after it is launched, but because it is not free for me to operate this, I may choose to end it depending on cost, usage, etc. For now, you can assume that I will be running this feature indefinitely unless the costs become too much to bear.

ETA: 1-3 months, highly dependent on time it takes to upload all media to cloud

Web Calendar

While there is already an external calendar that can be used in gmail, icloud, etc. sometimes you just want to quickly check the air date of an episode. The web calendar will show all of the air dates for shows that have been added from requests, or are currently in plex and thus being tracked. A potential additional feature of this would include adding upcoming movie release date in theaters, or the release of the blu-ray for a requested movie.

ETA: 1-2 months, depending on additional features added.

Personal Notifications

While having the current notification system is great, it can get tiresome to get spammed with notifications that you might not have any interest in. The new notification system will allow you to select which shows or movies that you want notifications for. Whether that is only movies and shows that you requested, or subscribing to notifications for certain shows or even all of them. The way in which multiple similar notifications will be announced will also change.

For example, instead of receiving a separate notification for each episode when an entire season or show is added, you will see on saying the over added status. (Season 1 added, Episodes 1,2,3,4 of Season 2 added).

ETA: 3+ months, because this requires a complete rewrite, other upcoming features will be prioritized.
